### HTTP 請求
GET `/private/v1/asset/sub-member/transfer/list`

### 請求參數
| 參數 | 是否必須 | 類型 | 說明|
|:----- |:-------|:-----|------ |
|transferId |false |string |UUID,全局唯一 |
|coin |false |string |幣種類型 |
|[status](../asset/enum#transferstatus) |false |string |劃轉狀態 |
|startTime |false |integer |開始時間戳（秒） |
|endTime |false |integer |結束時間戳（秒） |
|[direction](../asset/enum#assetdirection) |false |integer |翻頁 |
|limit |false |integer |記錄條數|
|cursor |false |string |游標信息：用來分頁|

### 響應參數
| 參數 | 類型 | 說明|
|:----- |:-----|----- |
|list |array|Object |
|> transferId |string |Transfer ID |
|> coin |string |Transferred coin |
|> amount |string |Transferred amount |
|> userId |int32 |Account |
|> subUserId |int32 |Sub Account |
|> timestamp |string |Transfer created timestamp (ms) |
|> [status](../asset/enum#transferstatus) |string |Transfer status |
|> [type](../asset/enum#transfertype) |**true** |string |Determines the direction of transfer |
|cursor |string|Refer to the `cursor` request parameter |

---

### 請求示例

```shell
GET /private/v1/asset/sub-member/transfer/list HTTP/1.1
Host: openapi-testnet.zoomex.com
X-BAPI-SIGN: XXXXX
X-BAPI-API-KEY: XXXXX
X-BAPI-TIMESTAMP: 1670988271299
X-BAPI-RECV-WINDOW: 50000
```
