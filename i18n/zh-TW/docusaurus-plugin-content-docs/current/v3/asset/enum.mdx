### transferType (`type`)
* `IN`&emsp;從主賬戶轉入到子賬戶
* `OUT`&emsp;從子賬戶轉出到主賬戶

### assetDirection (`direction`)
* `Prev`
* `Next`
* `first`
* `last`
* `jump`

## transferStatus (`status`)
* `STATUS_UNKNOWN`&emsp;未知
* `SUCCESS`&emsp;成功
* `PENDING`&emsp;处理中
* `FAILED`&emsp;失败
